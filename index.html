MATERIALS & FORM

Worn close.
Crafted to last.

Aether's body is precision-milled from a single block of grade-5 titanium — the same alloy used in surgical implants and aerospace frames. It holds its edge, keeps its finish, and resists everything daily life throws at it.

The face is a 4mm synthetic sapphire disc, the second-hardest transparent material on earth. Scratch it if you can.

Available in three finishes — Raw Titanium, Midnight PVD, and 18k Liquid Gold — with a hand-finished chain designed by artisans in Tokyo.
